For restaurant owners, who want to easily manage their menu items, OrderItUp is a web-based service that provides people who work in the restaurant industry with a system to keep track of and organize their menu items, as they update and change. Unlike other interactive online menus, our product provides a more efficient system that organizes items based on the needs of the restaurant owner as well as providing a place for collecting and storing.
